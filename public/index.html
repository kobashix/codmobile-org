Velikan Boss Guide

Building 21 - Solo Strategy

Phase 1: The Approach

Velikan spawns on the 3rd floor laboratory. Do NOT engage until you clear the Shadow Company bots. He uses an RGL-80, so stay away from tight corridors.

Phase 2: The Damage

Use the ZRG 20mm (Anti-Vehicle Mag) or thermites. He has high armor but is slow. Rotate between cover constantly.

Phase 3: The Loot

Defeating him drops the RGL-80 (Contraband) and the "Velikan's Keycard" which opens the Armory room on the 1st floor.
